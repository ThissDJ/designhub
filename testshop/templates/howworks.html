FAQ


For Designers With No Product But Ideas


1. What's next after leaving my contact?

A: Our team will contact you within 2 working days to arrange in-person meeting.


2.When will I receive the funds?

A: Our team will discuss details with you during the meeting. Funds will be released within 1 week after the meeting.


3. Do I have to sign a contract with you when I get the fund? What's the main content of that contract?

A: Yes,a contract will be signed for mutual interest. We guarantee you own 100% of your design and brand.


4. How much is the funds?

A:It's an item-based funding. We will fund designer for specific designs.


5: What should be my role in manufacturing?

A: Designers will be responsible for monitoring the process of manufacturing. Our team will provide assistance during the whole process.


6: Any cost of selling my designs on Design Hub?

A: $0 cost of selling on Design Hub.


7: What are your criteria in choosing designs?

A: Originality.
